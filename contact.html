Getting here

The directions.

The clinic is at Double Row, Seaton Delaval, NE25 0PP — a converted period building with a quiet treatment room upstairs. The door is plain; look for the brass “Refined” plate to the left.

By car: 10 minutes from Whitley Bay (A193 / A190), 15 from Tynemouth and Cramlington, 25 from Newcastle city centre via the A19. Free on-street parking directly outside.

By Metro: closest station is Whitley Bay on the Yellow Line — a 5-minute taxi or a 15-minute bus (Arriva 52 or 308) from there.

Nearby: we see regulars from Whitley Bay, Monkseaton, Shiremoor, Tynemouth, North Shields, Cramlington, Blyth, Bedlington, Morpeth, Gosforth, Jesmond and across greater Newcastle.

Your first visit

What happens
when you arrive.

01
A kettle on, not a clipboard
You’re greeted properly. There’s no reception queue, no hard sell, and the clinic is yours for the duration of your appointment.
02
Consultation, then treatment plan
Rachel works through your concerns, medical history and goals before anything is decided. If treatment is the right answer, you’ll have a clear plan and honest pricing before any needle is involved.
03
Two-week review, built in
Every injectable treatment includes a 14-day review. Any small tweaks on anti-wrinkle are on the house — that’s how you get a result that looks like you, just on a good day.
