The approach

What to expect.

Aqualyx is a non-surgical injectable for small pockets of stubborn, diet-resistant fat. A course of 2–4 sessions is usually recommended for best results. Most commonly used on the chin and jawline area.

Areas we treat: Chin · Jawline · Small body areas

Common questions

Good to know.

Am I a candidate?
Aqualyx works best on small, defined pockets of fat — not as a weight-loss treatment. Rachel will assess during consultation.
How many sessions?
Typically 2–4 sessions, 4–6 weeks apart.
Is there downtime?
Expect swelling and tenderness for 48–72 hours post-treatment.
