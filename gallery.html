Reading the pictures

What to look for.

A good before-and-after is quieter than people expect. We’re not trying to make you into someone else — we’re making small, structural changes that settle back into the way you already look.

Lip filler: look for hydration and definition, not volume. A good result restores the border and the cupid’s bow without changing the shape of the mouth. Natural lip treatments at Refined use 0.5–1ml of Juvederm Vycross and settle over two weeks.

Profile balancing: cheek, chin and jaw filler should bring the lower face into proportion with the upper — particularly for clients with a recessive chin or tired mid-face. The result reads as a better photograph, not a different face.

Profhilo & polynucleotides: the change is skin quality, not sculpt. Look for brightness, a smoother texture and the “bounce” coming back into the cheeks and jawline.

Microneedling: scar softening and pigmentation fading are slow — a course of three sessions six weeks apart is realistic. Results keep developing for three months after the final session.

On honesty
