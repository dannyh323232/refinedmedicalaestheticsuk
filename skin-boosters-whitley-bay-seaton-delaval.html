Common questions

Good to know.

How many sessions?
A standard Profhilo course is two sessions, four weeks apart, with a maintenance session every 4–6 months.
What's the difference vs filler?
Skin boosters don't add volume — they improve the underlying quality of your skin.
Can I combine with other treatments?
Yes. Boosters pair beautifully with anti-wrinkle or microneedling. Rachel will build you a plan.
