Cookie Policy

Last updated: 27 April 2026

This page lists every cookie and similar technology used on refinedmedicalaesthetics.uk, what each one does, and how long it lasts. We use the cookie banner (which you saw when you arrived on the site) to let you choose which categories you accept. Strictly necessary cookies are always on because the site can't function without them; everything else is opt-in.

Open cookie preferences

What's a cookie?

A cookie is a small text file stored on your device when you visit a website. Cookies and similar technologies (like local storage and pixels) help websites work, remember your preferences, measure how they're used, and (where you've consented) tailor advertising to you. The UK Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulations (PECR) require us to ask for your consent before setting non-essential ones.

How to change or withdraw your consent

You can change your cookie choices at any time using the button below or the "Cookie preferences" link in the site footer. Your choices apply across the site and persist for 120 days, after which we'll ask again.

Open cookie preferences

You can also block cookies entirely in your browser settings. The Information Commissioner's Office has a guide on managing cookies in your browser. Note that disabling strictly necessary cookies will stop parts of the site working.

Third-party cookies — control at the source

Some cookies are set by third parties when you accept the relevant category. You can also control them directly with the providers themselves:

  • Google Analytics — opt-out browser add-on
  • Google Ads — ad settings
  • Meta (Facebook / Instagram) — ad preferences

Updates

We update this page when we add or change a cookie. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent change.
