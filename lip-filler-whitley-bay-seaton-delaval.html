Common questions

Good to know.

How much filler do I need?
Most first lip treatments are 0.5ml. Rachel will never sell you more than your lips can carry.
Will my lips look done?
No. The Refined house style is soft, natural definition — not a traced lip.
Can I dissolve it later?
Yes. Hyaluronic lip filler is always reversible.
