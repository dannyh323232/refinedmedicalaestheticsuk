Common questions

Good to know.

Will it make my face wider?
Done properly, no. Rachel uses projection, not volume, to avoid a wider appearance.
How long does it last?
Typically 12–18 months for cheek filler.
How many ml do I need?
Usually 1–2ml across both cheeks. Your consultation determines the exact plan.
