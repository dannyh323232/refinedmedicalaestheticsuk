Rachel’s treatment approach

Refined focuses on suitability first. Advanced anti-wrinkle areas are planned around anatomy, movement, goals and what will still look natural in day-to-day life.

What happens at consultation

Your consultation covers the area of concern, medical history, suitability, pricing, expected timeline and whether anti-wrinkle treatment is actually the right route.

Safety and suitability

Advanced anti-wrinkle treatment is not suitable for everyone and some areas need more caution than standard treatment. Rachel will only proceed where it is clinically appropriate.
