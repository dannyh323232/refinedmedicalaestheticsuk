## 2. Patterns that likely drive conversion
1. **Hero blocks that combine promise + proof** – Each site pairs an emotional headline (“World Class Care”, “Raising the Bar in Aesthetics”) with immediate trust cues (awards, regulatory links, review count). This reduces bounce before explaining treatments.
2. **Secondary navigation for concerns or devices** – Tabs or pills ("Fine Lines & Wrinkles", "Hyperpigmentation") let visitors self-select pathways without scrolling through generic copy.
3. **Visible clinical credibility** – Bios/headshots, named surgeons, CQC badges, or “Performed by world-class experts” statements keep safety front and centre.
4. **Offer engines & memberships** – Subscription pricing (Thérapie), free consult valuations (Ever/Body), and referral credits (SkinSpirit) make acting now tangible.
5. **Social proof embedded in flow** – Before/after sliders and review snippets appear between sections rather than on separate pages, reinforcing trust at every stage.
6. **Booking funnels mirror retail checkout** – Book buttons open lightweight multi-step forms (location → concern → provider) and confirm via SMS/email; international sites also allow pre-paying for facials.
7. **Mobile-first card stacks** – Content compresses into swipeable cards (treatment tiles, testimonials). Sticky footer CTAs (“Call / WhatsApp / Book”) appear on scroll.
8. **Treatment detail pages behave like mini-landing pages** – Each page includes hero photo, quick facts (Downtime, Price, Duration), device explainer, candidacy checklist, FAQ, and booking CTA repeated at top + bottom.

## 3. Exact changes to apply on Refined site
| Area | Change | Why (ties to benchmark) |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Hero section | Replace current static hero with split layout: left = bold promise (“Advanced medical aesthetics, nurse-led in Stockton Heath”), right = trust stack (CQC link, GPhC number, “500+ 5★ Google reviews”). Add dual CTAs: **Book consultation** + **See results**. | Mirrors Cadogan/Ever/Body structure that combines promise + proof and gives choice of conversion or exploration.
| Navigation & concerns menu | Add top-level chips under hero for “Skin quality”, “Injectables”, “Body contouring”, “Women’s health”. Each chip anchors to relevant section. | Imitates Ever/Body & Thérapie concern filters, reducing cognitive load for first-time visitors.
| Offer/subscription banner | Introduce a slim, dismissible ribbon advertising a limited offer (e.g., “Spring Glow: 3-skin-pen package save 15%”) that links to a checkout-ready landing page. | Aligns with Thérapie’s constant promo visibility, driving urgency without clutter.
| Clinical credibility block | Insert section with Rachel’s credentials, NHS role, and any partner injectors plus professional logos (NMC, BCAM). Use headshot grid with short expertise bullets and “Meet your practitioner” CTA. | Benchmarks show named experts materially boost trust.
| Review + before/after carousel | Build a combined slider that alternates Google review snippets (with name + location) and before/after images with consent. Place midway down homepage and duplicate on relevant treatment pages. | PHI & SkinSpirit embed proof between sections to reinforce decision-making.
| Treatment page template | Redesign treatment detail pages to include quick facts panel (Downtime, Session length, Investment), treatment overview, device explanation, candidacy checklist, FAQ accordion, and sticky “Book/WhatsApp” CTA. | Matches Ever/Body/PHI “mini-landing page” approach proven to convert.
| Booking funnel | Add multi-step Typeform or embedded form: Step 1 choose goal, Step 2 pick preferred slot/channel, Step 3 capture contact + consent → auto-confirm email with calendar file. Surface WhatsApp click-to-chat for instant questions. | Competitors mimic e-commerce checkout; this reduces friction vs. current single contact form.
| Mobile UX | Implement sticky footer bar on mobile with icons for Call, WhatsApp, Book, Directions. Convert long paragraphs into card stacks with concise headings for scannability. | International leaders prioritise thumb-friendly controls and card layouts, improving on-the-go conversions.
| Membership/referral | Launch simple “Glow Plan” (e.g., £50/month credit + quarterly skin review) and referral reward (£25 credit for both parties). Promote via homepage tile and email follow-up. | SkinSpirit’s referral + Ever/Body’s memberships keep LTV high and prompt word-of-mouth.
| Content cadence | Add “What’s trending” blog/tile updated monthly (e.g., new device, seasonal prep) with CTA to relevant treatment. | Thérapie keeps site feeling alive with trending modules; improves SEO freshness and returning visits.
